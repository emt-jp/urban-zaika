## Review Request Timing Strategy

| Timing | Method | Message |
|--------|--------|---------|
| At the table | QR code card | Scan to review (print review-qr.html) |
| Same day (evening) | LINE/WhatsApp | Thank you + review link |
| 3 days later (if no review) | LINE | Gentle reminder with photo of their dish |
| Weekly | Google Post | "We love hearing from you!" + review link |

## Tips for Getting More Reviews

1. **Ask at the right moment** — when the customer compliments the food or says thank you
2. **Make it easy** — QR code on the table, no login required
3. **Respond to every review** — within 24 hours shows you care
4. **Never offer incentives** — Google prohibits discounts/freebies for reviews
5. **Train staff** — teach them to say "If you enjoyed your meal, we'd appreciate a Google review!"
   - Japanese: 「もしお気に召しましたら、Googleの口コミをいただけると嬉しいです」
   - Urdu: 「اگر آپ کو کھانا پسند آیا تو گوگل ریویو دے دیں」
