Our Story

A Tradition of Flavor

❅

At Urban Zaika, we bring the rich culinary heritage of India and Pakistan to the vibrant streets of Tokyo. Our chefs craft each dish using time-honored family recipes passed down through generations, ensuring every bite is an authentic journey of flavor. We are proudly 100% halal certified, using only the finest ingredients sourced with care and prepared with devotion.

From the aromatic biryanis of Hyderabad to the robust karahis of Lahore, our menu celebrates the diverse and extraordinary cuisines of the subcontinent — all in the heart of Tokyo.
